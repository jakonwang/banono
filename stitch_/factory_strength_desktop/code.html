Uncompromising Quality Assurance

Our 3-step inspection process is the cornerstone of our manufacturing excellence, ensuring every product meets international standards.

IQC: Incoming Materials

Raw material screening for purity and compliance with global environmental standards like RoHS and REACH before production begins.

IPQC: In-Process Control

Continuous monitoring of the production line with real-time automated optical inspection (AOI) to detect and correct deviations instantly.
