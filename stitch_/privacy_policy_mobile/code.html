Introduction

Welcome to Qiaotian Enterprise. We are committed to protecting your personal data and your right to privacy. This policy explains how we treat personal information when you visit our manufacturing portal or engage in our global export services.

As a leading 3C accessories manufacturer, transparency is the cornerstone of our industrial operations. This document outlines the protocols we follow to ensure your data remains as secure as our manufacturing processes.

Data Collection

TYPES OF DATA COLLECTED

  • check_circle Professional Identity: Name, company affiliation, and job title for procurement verification.

Security Measures

Industrial-Grade Encryption

We implement robust technical and organizational security measures designed to protect the security of any personal information we process. This includes AES-256 encryption for data at rest and TLS 1.3 for all data in transit.

Our internal access controls are strictly governed by the Principle of Least Privilege (PoLP), ensuring that only authorized procurement and legal personnel have access to specific datasets.
